About me

Hello, I'm Kris!

I studied Biology at Bielefeld University, and am looking into the humanities. Language learning is one of my greatest passions, and I am deeply committed to international cooperation and intercultural programmes.

I am a Referent of the Department of International Affairs of the General Students' Committee (AStA) of Bielefeld University. I advocate for the interests of international students and work closely with university institutions and organisations such as the International Student Council (ISR) and the Erasmus Student Network (ESN).

I am also a member of the Board of the Language Centre (FSZ) of Bielefeld University and of the Committee for Studies and Teaching. Additionally, I am a mentor in the International Mentoring Programme at Bielefeld University, supporting newly arriving international students in settling into life in Bielefeld and at the university.
